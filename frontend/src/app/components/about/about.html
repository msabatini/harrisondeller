Artist Statement

Harrison Deller is a contemporary artist and musician whose work explores the intersection of visual and auditory expression. Through painting and music, Harrison creates immersive experiences that challenge conventional boundaries between artistic mediums.

With a background in both fine arts and music composition, Harrison's paintings often reflect rhythmic patterns and harmonic structures, while his musical compositions draw inspiration from visual landscapes and color theory.

Background

Harrison Deller (1993 Quito, Ecuador) earned his Bachelor's degree in Painting from the Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD) in Savannah, Georgia, USA in 2017. Subsequently, in 2022, he completed his Master's degree in Painting and Drawing at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Throughout his career, Harrison has received several awards and recognitions, including being on the dean's list at SCAD (2013-2017) and the institution's merit scholarship. In 2019, he won the studio award at Mana Contemporary Miami.
